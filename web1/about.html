Our Story

How it all started

It all started in a small apartment with one laptop and a lot of coffee. Our founder, Ali Hassan, had spent years helping friends set up their websites and realised there was a real gap small businesses were either paying too much or getting stuck trying to do it themselves. In 2022, we came together as a team of seven designers, developers and a content writer with one shared goal: make professional web development accessible to every business. Today we've delivered websites for bakeries, photographers, law firms and clothing brands. Every project is built with the same care like it's our own business on the line.
